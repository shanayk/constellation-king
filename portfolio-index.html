About & Résumé

I'm an RF engineer at SpaceX, where I've been working since 2020 on satellite communications systems — including phased array antennas for LEO constellations. Before that I spent three years at Cisco Meraki leading wireless hardware quality and building out an in-house RF test lab, and a year at Supply Inc. developing OTA wireless charging solutions.

My career in RF started with an internship at Apple on the iPhone RF Systems Engineering team. In 2022 I co-authored an IEEE paper on incident power density of electrically-large planar phased arrays communicating with non-geostationary satellites — a problem that sits at the intersection of antenna engineering, EMF compliance, and the realities of constantly scanning LEO beams.

Patents

Smart Rectenna Design for Passive Wireless Power Harvesting

Granted

A system enabling passive power harvesting from ambient electromagnetic emissions using a smart rectenna with automatic frequency response tuning. Combines a tunable high-pass filter and voltage multiplier rectifier with an ultra-wideband antenna, using a lookup table to bias tunable components toward the frequency region of maximum power spectral density in incident energy signals.

US20210384775A1 Cisco Technology Inc Filed Aug 2018 Published Dec 2021 View Patent ↗

Enhancing Indoor Positioning Using RF Multilateration and Optical Sensing

Granted

A system combining radio frequency multilateration with machine vision for high-accuracy indoor positioning. Wireless access points determine an approximate location area from broadcast signals; camera-based image analysis then refines that position and classifies detected targets — significantly reducing computational requirements compared to purely RF-based or vision-based approaches alone.

Homebrew VNA

Active

A two-port vector network analyzer built from scratch using an Si5351 clock generator, a pair of directional couplers wound on ferrite cores, and an RTL-SDR as the detector. Covers 10 kHz – 200 MHz. Calibration routines written in Python using the 12-term SOLT model.
